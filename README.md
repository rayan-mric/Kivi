## **1-Point Combinations**

* **Two pairs**
  Two dice share one value, and another two dice share a different value.

* **Three of a kind**
  Three dice share the same value.

* **Little straight**
  Any **four** dice form a sequence of consecutive numbers.

* **Full house**
  Three dice show one value and two dice show another.

---

## **2-Point Combinations**

* **Four of a kind**
  Four dice show the same value.

* **Large straight**
  Any **five** dice form a consecutive sequence.

* **All even**
  All six dice show even numbers.

* **All odd**
  All six dice show odd numbers.

* **12 or fewer**
  The sum of all dice is **12 or less**.

* **30 or more**
  The sum of all dice is **30 or more**.

---

## **3-Point Combinations**

* **Three pairs**
  The six dice form three separate pairs.

* **Two times three of a kind**
  Three dice show one value, and the remaining three dice show another.

* **Four of a kind and a pair**
  Four dice share one value, and the remaining two dice share another.

## **Special Combinations**
